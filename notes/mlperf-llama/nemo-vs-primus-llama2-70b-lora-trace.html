Action items for Primus

1) Eliminate 191 ms idleHigh-impact

Enable DataLoader workers (num_workers=2-8, persistent_workers=True, prefetch_factor=2) with multiprocessing_context="spawn" to avoid fork-after-CUDA deadlock.

2) Keep TE op-fuser ONConfirmed-good

Do not disable op-fuser for parity. It keeps standalone transpose at 21 ms vs 1232 ms.

3) Reduce FP8 amax historyMedium-impact

Align bridge precision with NeMo: fp8_amax_history_len=4, fp8_amax_compute_algo=most_recent.

4) Match RCCL channel tuningLow-priority

Add NCCL_MIN_CTAS=32, NCCL_MIN_P2P_NCHANNELS=32, NCCL_NCHANNELS_PER_NET_PEER=32; keep TORCH_NCCL_HIGH_PRIORITY=1.

Traces from torch.profiler with record_shapes=false, with_stack=false, one active step/rank. Parsed via .cursor/skills/gpu-trace-analysis/scripts/full_breakdown.py on NeMo rank 4 and Primus rank 2.

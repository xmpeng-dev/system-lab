**AI Infrastructure Architect — Training Systems & Agent-Driven Optimization**

## About

I design the systems that make large-scale training efficient and agent-optimizable:
performance observability layers, cross-backend optimization interfaces, and
structured engineering knowledge that bridges hardware understanding with
automated tuning.

My work sits at the intersection of distributed training architecture,
hardware-aware optimization, and AI agent integration — focusing not just on
making things fast, but on making the optimization process itself systematic
and reproducible.

---

## What I Do

- **Performance Observability** — Design telemetry and diagnostic systems that
  surface actionable performance semantics from distributed training workloads,
  enabling both human engineers and AI agents to identify and resolve bottlenecks
- **Cross-Backend Optimization** — Architect unified optimization interfaces
  across heterogeneous hardware (NVIDIA / AMD), abstracting backend-specific
  behaviors into structured, tunable surfaces
- **Knowledge Engineering** — Encode hardware-aware optimization experience into
  reusable, machine-consumable form — bridging the gap between implicit
  engineering intuition and explicit, executable optimization rules
- **Cross-Layer Trade-off Decisions** — Make the architectural calls that span
  parallelism strategy, precision policy, communication topology, and memory
  management — the kind of joint optimization that requires full-stack
  understanding
